Hello, world! A pleasure to meet you.

My name is Matthew R. Vaden. I am a Washington, D.C. based web developer, perpetual learner, community servant, a traveler, a lover of the arts and live performance, an ultimate frisbee enthusiast and a total shameless nerd.

Previously, I worked government, youth economic development, and higher education. During Spring 2016, I was enrolled in General Assembly's Web Development Immersive (WDI 9). Currently, I am a software engineer and scrum master on a GIS federal project with Leidos.

Some of my technical interests include progressive web development, UI design, data visualization, open source, and supporting local meetups and other not-for-profit intiatives. But I tend to get distracted a lot and end up working on a number of other things.
